Guinea pigs

Guinea Pigs are one of the best animals to have. Guinea pigs are cute. They know how to do a lot of tricks. They also like vegetables. They are very smart.

Guinea Pig Facts

Some facts about guinea pigs is, they can be wild animals. They arent pigs and they dont come from Guinea. A male guinea pig is called a boar and a female guinea pig is called a sow. It is important for guinea pigs to chew on something because their teeth grow constantly. They can live between 7 and 9 years. A guinea pig can be three hours old to run. Guinea pigs nails also grow constantly so it is important to clip them. When they are happy, they jump up in the air. It is called "popcorning" Guinea Pigs are one of the most popular house pets. They are also one of the most adorable animals. They are smart and can learn a lot in one day. Guinea pigs can be wild animals or they could be kept as pets.

Diet

Guinea Pigs eat vegetables like carrots, lettuce, spinach, kale, or parsley. They eat fruits like grapes, strawberries, watermelon, apples, oranges, and more. Another thing they eat is Timothy hay, pellets, zucchini, and sweet potatoes. If you feed them to much, it can cause stomach problems. You are supposed to feed guinea pigs in the morning and at night.

Tricks

Guinea pigs are very smart so they can do a lot of tricks. They can stand up, walk around in a circle, shake, and beg. They can also go up to you when you call their name. They can follow you, or even play dead. One of the most useful and popular tricks is learning how to use a litter box. It takes a lot of patience to teach them. You always want to reward them with food when they do a trick.

When you First get them

When you first get a guinea pig, you have to let it get used to it's surroundings. It is being introduced to a new place and has to get used to it. When you first get one and have another guinea pig,you don't introduce them to each other right away. You have to wait at least 1-2 weeks before you introduce them or else, they will possibly fight. Start off by feeding them little amounts of food. Let them get used to you before you pick them up. Start off by giving them something to hide in just incase they feel scared. After about a week, try picking them up. They can either be scared, or more trusting depending on what you do when you first get them.
